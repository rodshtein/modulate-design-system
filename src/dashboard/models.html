Velma-2 STT Batch

Velma 2's powerful and flexible batch transcription model, supporting over 70 languages with features such as speaker diarization, accent detection, emotion detection, PII/PHI tagging, and more.

Endpoint: https://modulate-developer-apis.com/api/velma-2-stt-batch

Type: batch. Accepted media formats: aac, aiff, flac, mov, mp3, mp4, ogg, opus, wav, webm. Concurrency quota: 5 concurrent requests. Monthly usage quota: 1,000 hours. Cost: $0.03 per hour.

Velma-2 STT Batch — English Fast

English-only batch transcription with extremely high throughput and automatic capitalization and punctuation. Opus format audio only. Ideal for high-scale batch processing.

Endpoint: https://modulate-developer-apis.com/api/velma-2-stt-batch-english-vfast

Velma-2 STT Streaming

Real-time streaming transcription via WebSocket with full voice-native understanding: multilingual support, speaker diarization, emotion, accent, and PII/PHI tagging.

Endpoint: https://modulate-developer-apis.com/api/velma-2-stt-streaming
